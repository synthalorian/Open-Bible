# Privacy Policy for Open Bible App

**Last Updated: February 28, 2026**

## Introduction

Open Bible ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **User Preferences**: App settings, font size, theme preferences
- **Bookmarks**: Bible verses you bookmark for quick access
- **Reading Plans**: Progress on reading plans you create or follow
- **Prayer Journal**: Prayer requests and notes you enter
- **Highlights**: Verses you highlight within the app

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent reading
- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version (for compatibility)
- **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash data to improve app stability

## How We Use Your Information

1. **App Functionality**: To provide Bible reading, bookmarking, and study features
2. **Personalization**: To remember your preferences and reading position
3. **Improvement**: To analyze usage and improve the app experience
4. **Offline Support**: To cache Bible content for offline reading

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: All personal data (bookmarks, notes, preferences) is stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync**: We do not upload your personal data to our servers
- **Optional API**: Bible content is fetched from api.bible when internet is available

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **API.Bible**: For fetching Bible translations and content
- **Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics (optional)

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- We do not sell or share your personal information
- We implement industry-standard security practices

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
- Export your bookmarks and notes (feature coming soon)
- Opt-out of analytics in app settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
